About Us



WHY WE EXIST

Empowering organisations, to empower people. All South Africans are interconnected and share one future. We must, therefore, collaborate and draw on systems thinking to have a Positive Exponential Impact on our country’s development.

OUR VISION

We aspire to be the leading organisation in South Africa for the development of innovative solutions that enable organisations to meaningfully contribute towards socio-economic empowerment and upliftment in our country.

  • World-Class B-BBEE Advisory Services.
  • Skills Development and Training.
  • Socio-Economic Development Opportunities.
  • Local and International Business.
  • Networks/Partnerships.
  • Provision of Event and Training Facilities.
  • Promoting Localisation through Intermediate Inputs.
